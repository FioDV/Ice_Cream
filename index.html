Oreo Cookie Ice Cream

Interesting Facts

Oreo ice cream, later named Cookies 'n Cream, was invented by dairy plant manager Shirley Seas and dairy science students Joe Leedom and Joe Van Treeck. It combines the creamy texture of vanilla ice cream with oreo cookies.
